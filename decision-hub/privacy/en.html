Privacy Policy — Decision Hub (Karar Merkezi)

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Decision Hub (“the app”), published by Burak Meşe (“we”, “us”), handles information when you use our Android app (com.decisionhub.app).

1. Summary

Decision Hub provides decision tools such as a spin wheel, dice, coin flip, and more. No account or login is required. Most data is stored locally on your device. The free version shows ads through Google AdMob. Optional premium removes ads.

2. Information we collect

Data we collect directly:

  • We do not collect registration or profile data.
  • Tool drafts, history, preferences, and settings are stored on your device.
  • Optional daily quote notifications are scheduled locally only; not sent to our servers.

Third parties (ads & store): Google AdMob may process, for example:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address and device information
  • App interactions related to ads
  • Diagnostic and performance data for ad delivery

Premium purchases are handled by Google Play; we do not access your payment details. See AdMob privacy and Google Privacy Policy.

3. How we use information

  • Operate the app and save preferences on your device
  • Keep history and tool data locally
  • Display and measure ads via AdMob
  • Verify premium purchases (Google Play)
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Legal bases (EEA/UK)

Where applicable, processing is based on legitimate interests (app operation, advertising) and consent where required for personalized ads (manageable in device and Google ad settings).

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Google (AdMob, Play services) may process data under their policies.

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. Contact us if you believe a child provided personal data.

7. Your choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Android Settings → Google → Ads
  • Reset advertising ID: same menu
  • Disable notifications: in-app Settings or system notification settings
  • Delete local data: clear app data or uninstall

8. Security & retention

We use reasonable security measures. Local data remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall. Ad partners retain data per their policies.

9. International transfers

Google/AdMob may process data in countries where they operate.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use means acceptance.
