Privacy Policy — Arrow Flow

Last updated: June 26, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Arrow Flow (“the app”), published by Burak Meşe (“we”, “us”), handles information when you use our Android game (com.burak.arrowflow).

1. Summary

Arrow Flow is an offline-capable puzzle game. The app uses Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob and Google may collect data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy. We do not operate user accounts or a separate login in this app.

2. Information we collect

Data collected by us directly: We do not require registration. Game progress may be stored locally on your device (e.g. level progress, settings).

Data collected by third parties (advertising): Google AdMob may collect or receive, for example:

  • Advertising ID (AAID)
  • IP address and device information
  • App interactions related to ads
  • Diagnostic and performance data for ad delivery

See AdMob & privacy for details.

3. How we use information

  • To run the game and save your preferences on your device
  • To display and measure ads through AdMob
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal bases (EEA/UK users)

Where applicable, processing is based on legitimate interests (app operation, advertising) and your consent where required for personalized ads (manageable in device and Google ad settings).

5. Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed by Google (AdMob, Play services) under their policies.

6. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will assist with removal requests.

7. Your choices

  • Opt out of personalized ads: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • Reset advertising ID: same menu on your device
  • Uninstall the app to remove local app data

8. Security & retention

We use reasonable measures to protect data. Ad partners retain data per their policies. Local game data remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall.

9. International transfers

AdMob/Google may process data in countries where they operate, with safeguards as described in Google’s documentation.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
